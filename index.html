Project 1: EWD Humanitarian Aid Mission

In response to the unique challenges of an underdeveloped village with limited access, we engineered a turbine blade using Computer-Aided Design (CAD) to optimize its performance. Through collaborative efforts, we aimed to harness renewable energy efficiently, addressing the village's power needs in a sustainable and accessible manner.
